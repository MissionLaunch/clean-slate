You Can Succeed WITH a Criminal History Record

You may be able to seal your criminal record. When a DC criminal record is sealed, the government deletes or limits access to it. A sealed record can generally not be used against you if are applying for a job, in other background checks or in certain legal proceedings. A lawyer can help determine if you are eligible.

This website can help you decide if you want to seal your record and, if you do, connect you with a lawyer who can help.
